What Is a Panoramic Room?

A panoramic room is a hotel room designed to maximise wide, immersive views using larger windows, corner positioning, higher floors, or floor-to-ceiling glass. Unlike standard rooms, the focus is on the breadth and quality of the outlook.

Typical features

  • Wide or wraparound windows
  • Corner room positioning
  • More natural light
  • Higher floor locations

Why panoramic rooms cost more

Hotels usually have only a few rooms with truly wide views, which makes them premium inventory.

Is it worth it?

If the view is part of the experience — romantic trips, photography, relaxing stays — panoramic rooms can be worth paying extra for.
