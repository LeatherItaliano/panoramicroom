Panoramic Room vs Ocean View Room

These two hotel room categories sound similar but describe different things.

Panoramic room

A panoramic room focuses on a wide, immersive view regardless of landscape — city, mountains, or ocean.

Ocean view room

An ocean view room simply means the ocean is visible. The view might be partial, distant, or only visible from one angle.

Which is better?

  • Choose panoramic for the “wow factor” and natural light.
  • Choose ocean view if water visibility is your priority.
